About Us.

Sun sand & surf amidst a palette of blissful surprises and serenity. Popular and known as Radhika Beach Resort, this beautiful and unparalleled dream location is situated in the very picturesque seaside town of Diu, a city in Diu district in the union territory of Daman and Diu, India. Diu District is an island off the south coast of Gujarat's Kathiawar peninsula, separated from the mainland only by a tidal creek. We are located at the eastern end of the island, which like the union territory, is replete with Portuguese culture and contemporary livingy.
